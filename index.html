World Happiness Report

Happiness, while it may be a subjective concept for many, is an important factor in determining the well-being of a country. One method in which we have produced to measure happiness in an objection fashion is through the use of the World Happiness Index. The World Happiness Report is a landmark survey of the state of global happiness. It ranks 156 countries by how happy their citizens perceive themselves to be. The report is produced by the United Nations Sustainable Development Solutions Network in partnership with the Ernesto Illy Foundation.
